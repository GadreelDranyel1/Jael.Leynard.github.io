About Me

Bonjour! I'm Leynard Chequito.

               I'm a student, a simple artist, and a daydreamer who aspires to become a web designer. I'm currently studying BS in Information Technology at Mindoro State University, where I'm gaining the knowledge and skills needed to create beautiful and functional websites. As a simple artist, I love to experiment with colors, shapes, and textures to create visually stunning designs that engage and captivate users. My daydreaming tendencies often lead me to think outside the box, which is essential when it comes to designing unique and innovative websites. In my free time, you'll often find me browsing the web and exploring new design trends and technologies. It's a pleasure to meet you, and I'm excited to see what the future holds for my career as a web designer!
